More Details

Can I upgrade my plan?

Absolutely. You can change your chapter's plan up or down depending on your needs.

Can I cancel my plan?

You never sign any contacts with ConnectedGreek. If you are unhappy, we haven't done our job and we won't force you to stay. Our hunch is you'll keep coming back!

What are the payment structures?

If you want to pay monthly, semesterly, or annually you can. If you want to pay annually we have a 5% discount.

Do we need a credit card to sign up?

No. In addition to Visa, Mastercard, Discover and American Express we accept checks by mail.

Can you help us raise money for the site?

Absolutely! Contact us and we will set you up with a donation page to help raise the money for your site. Once you've earned enough to cover the setup fee and first month, your site will go live!
