ConnectedGreek | Fraternity and Sorority Websites

The best website for your chapter. Period.

Get in touch with your chapter like you never have before.

Professional Design

ConnectedGreek gives your chapter a unified, branded site to showcase your members. We have a team of qualified and seasoned professionals who will make you site match who your chapter represents, not just what is easiest.

We won't stop until you love it.

We don't want you to just be OK with your site. We want you to LOVE it. During the design process, we'll work with you until thats what happens. There is no limit to initial revisions.

Not some whack template

We don't just take a template and put your picture on it! We can have anything you want included in your design from auto-updating slideshows to donation forms. While all the other greek organizations on your campus have matching templates, show potential new members something different!

Easy to update

Design is great, but only if the content on your website is up to date. With a ConnectedGreek website you can give your members the ability to update pages on the website right through the browser. If you can write an email or edit a word document, you can update your website.

No software to install

Your website will be able to be updated from any computer with a web connection. You don't need to install any applications or extensions, it all works through all modern web browsers such as IE 7, IE 8, Firefox, Safari, or Google Chrome. ConnectedGreek recommends Google Chrome for the fastest experience.

Upload Photos, Publish Videos

Showcase your chapter using our picture uploader

Lots of room so you don't run out

All of our plans have plenty of room so you don't need to worry about running out when you are uploading photos. We'll make sure they are displayed with the highest quality and compression techniques so your photos download quickly and look great.

Tag Pictures

Write captions and tag members in photos so potential new members can start putting names to faces.

Recruitment Rules? Hide specific members

Some chapters have members they need to hide from photos and from the site during recruitment. With one click, all tagged pictures of any member can be disguised or removed. Once recruitment is over, bring them back!

Email Members, Alumni and Recruits

Our easy to use mailing lists make it simple to send out branded emails for your chapter. Keep members in the loop with chapter meetings and Alumni coming back to donate with email blasts.

Google Apps Email Addresses

We'll hook you up with Google Apps for your domain name. Have professional looking email addresses forward to your current account (e.g. president@chapterdomainname.com).

SMS, Email and internal accounments

With plenty of ways to contact your chapter, you'll be able to keep everyone on the same page. Enjoy point and click messaging to separate groups and member types so that everyone knows whats going on. Great for safety alerts, event reminders, time changes and spur of the moment plans!
